How Does It Work?


The process to create sofrware is really straightforward look at the flow below and click to get more information.


Assesment


During our assessment meeting you can tell me all about your idea and we can discuss all the available options to make it true. Creating an application from zero is not always the best option. This meeting will take as much time as we need so that we can get all the requirements and details to propose a great solution.

The Assesment meeting is completely free!

Analysis


After that meeting a period of analysis will take place were the requirements and details will be evaluated and processed to create a development proposal. During this time, we will have a back and forth of ideas and solutions that meet your budgetary needs and your solution goals.

Design


Design is the process where we create the internal and external structures that will be your idea.

Development


Development is a cyclical process where we will create your solution with code and creativity. As we create new parts of your solution, we can review them and pivot if necessary. This in known as an agile methodology where we can always cater to your needs and goals.

Deploy


1,2,3 launch! (rocket imagery here) this is the moment where we focus on pushing your application to market. Any issues or bugs will be squashed at no cost for the first month.
